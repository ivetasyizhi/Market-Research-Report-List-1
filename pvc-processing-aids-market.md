<p><p>PVC processing aids are specialized additives used to improve the processing efficiency and quality of polyvinyl chloride (PVC) during manufacturing. Additionally, advancements in polymer technology and the development of eco-friendly processing aids are shaping market trends.</p><p>Recent trends include the growing preference for high-performance processing aids that offer improved thermal stability and reduced energy consumption during production.
