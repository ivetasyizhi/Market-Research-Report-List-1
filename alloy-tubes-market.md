<p><ul><li>Seamless Alloy Tubes</li><li>Welded Alloy Tubes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Alloy Tubes market comprises two main types: Seamless Alloy Tubes and Welded Alloy Tubes. Seamless tubes are manufactured without welding, offering superior strength, uniformity, and resistance to high pressure and temperature, making them ideal for critical applications like oil and gas, aerospace, and power generation. Welded Alloy Tubes are formed by welding rolled alloy sheets, providing cost-effectiveness and versatility for less demanding applications such as construction, automotive, and general engineering. Both types cater to diverse industrial needs based on performance and budget requirements.</p></p>
